#### Alright, now I'll introduce you into the formation and commands section

Let's start with Commands.
##### The First will be STS.
STS is short for "Shoulder to Shoulder" which means you make a line side by side with your shoulders lining up but not touching, hence the name "Shoulder to Shoulder"
##### The Second will be STW.
STW is short for "Shoulder to Wall" which means you make a line side by side with your shoulders lining up but not touching with your back facing the wall. Hence the name "Shoulder to Wall".
##### The Third will be Left and Right face
In short it'll be L/R Face. Basically, you will be asked to turn 90 degrees to either left or right.
##### The Fourth will be SFL
SFL is short for Single File Line, which means you make a line but instead of side to side like STS, you will make a single line following the front of you like an ant, hence the name single file line.
##### The Fifth would be Wedge.
A Wedge is similar to a STS, but instead of side by side, you move to behind the person in front of you, and align your shoulder, depending on your position, for example. If you wedge on the right side, you will align your left shoulder to the back of the right shoulder of the person in front of you.
